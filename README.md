# Ascent
*Before we get into this, please note that this is a personal project and is not open to public contributions.*

## About
Are you tired of losing money? Do you want to keep more of your hard-earned dollars? Well, I can't fix that for you, but maybe you can see where it's all going.

Introducing **Ascent**!

Ascent is all about *clarity*. The more clarity can be provided for personal finances, the more one can grow their wealth. Powerful data analysis paired with colorful visuals power this goal!

Ascent is a powerful finance tracking application that uses tags in order to track exactly where you money goes. Using Ascent is simple. Whenever you make a purchase or pay a bill, just enter the transaction and enter one or more tags to categorize it. Then as time goes on you will be able to see exactly where your money goes and where it comes from.

## Important Notes
In order to track your financial performance, Ascent assumes that every dollar that you spend and earn is accounted for. Beyond that, transactions can be entered and categorized in whichever way suits your needs best. Personally I keep a 1:1 correspondence between my bank activity and my Ascent ledger, but as I said, you can enter them split up or together as long as every dollar is included. But don't worry, all of your data stays on your system and no analytics are collected in any way. **YOU** are in control of your data!
